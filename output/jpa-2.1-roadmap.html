#JPA 2.1 Development Roadmap

Under Construction... This page is modeled after the [JPA 2.0 development roadmap](jpa-2.0-roadmap.html). Reference that often while constructing this page...

Apache OpenJPA 2.x will be developed in trunk and will be a JPA 2.1 certified release of the JSR-338 specification.

Development Process¶

The process that OpenJPA will be following for developing JPA 2.1 functionality is documented here . The specific roadmap and iteration details will be documented on this Roadmap page.

Test Coverage¶

For a list of databases being tested and current status, checkout the JPA 2.1 Test Coverage page.
